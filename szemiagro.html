A site what I developed for an international agricultural machine dealer company. The page is based on a customized Joomla template with some additional extensions (Adsmanager, Joomfish, sh404SEF etc).

The main requirement was a simple, easily manageable interface, where the company can show it's current stock, the details of a selected machine (photos, description, price etc.) and information to the customers to how to reach them.

Szemi-agro has facebook integration to increase social media engagement, and google analytics to monitor and analyze to traffic of the site.

The content are available in 3 different languages (English, Hungarian, Romanian)
